About the web site

Sciecom lives on balder.lub.lu.se:/localdisk1/sciecom. The services has two virtual servers on that machine, www.sciecom.org and sciecom.lub.lu.se, where the latter is a sandbox for experimental purposes (currently used by sciecom staff for testing ideas concerning user interface and the structural design of the site). Most content on the site maintained using the Keystone Software.

There is one exception; the collection of articles & news distributed as Sciecom Info (available on http://www.sciecom.org/sciecominfo/) is a symbolic link from another directory /home/lubwww/lub/www/sciecom/sciecominfo imported using NFS from yoda:/export/lubwww.

The service is based on the Biblioteca1 service distributed by Indexdata. Sciecom is a founding member of the Keystone Hotel.
