Listings of papers by department and levels

There is a need for some departments, namely those that use XERXES for administrating the production of papers within their courses, to have specific interfaces for this purpose.

Xerxes provide such an interface though a script list-items.cgi. It has the URL http://theses.lub.lu.se/courses/list-items.cgi. By default it will deliver a boolean search for masters theses from the Department of Political Science (since this department was the first to ask for this feature)

The syntax of a query to list-items.cgi is as follows:

      http://theses.lub.lu.se/courses/list-items.cgi/<LU number>/<lvl>?status=<status>

where there parameters between < and > are defined as follows

Arguments to the list-items.cgi script
LU numberThe unique identifier of a department within Lund university given in Lucat and which is registered in XERXES, e.g., 012015000 for department of political science and 012009000 for Dept of Education.
lvlCourse level, i.e., A, B, C, D and Y. Please refer to the SVEP metadata model
statusAny of inprep, submitted and accepted for papers that are In preparation', Submitted and Accepted, respectively.

Examples

      http://theses.lub.lu.se/courses/list-items.cgi/012009000/C?status=inprep
      http://theses.lub.lu.se/courses/list-items.cgi/012009000/C?status=submitted
