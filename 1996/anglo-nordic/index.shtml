Note added 2004

Everything here is left as it was when I used these slides for a presentation I held 1996 at the 10th Anglo-Nordic Seminar. The theme of the Seminar this year was "networking".

I feel that everything here is obsolete. But maybe not: The problems we tried to address at that time are not solved. Yet. Nevertheless, a lot of of us then actually believed that there could be a search engine for the Internet which had the characteristics of a Library OPAC. We know now that such search engines will never ever be developed.
