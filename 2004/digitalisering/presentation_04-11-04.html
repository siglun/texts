St Laurentius Digital Manuscript Library

  • http://laurentius.lub.lu.se/
  • 67 volymer, eller 18.000 bilder (nästan 1 TB)
  • Liten databas, bara 67 poster, extremt detaljerad katalog http://laurentius.lub.lu.se/volumes/Mh_35/detailed/

Syfte:

Medieval manuscripts are complicated. Each manuscript is a unique individual with its own history. Often, not always, they contain several pieces of intellectual content, each of which may be well known in the sense that this content appear in many manuscripts from the same time, and this content may appear in printed editions today.
To catalogue medieval manuscripts requires a complex descriptive metadata schema, which is capable of capturing both the individual manuscript as work of art and as a unique blend of well known intellectual content.
That is, you should be able to search for works by (say) Boethius or Virgil. But you should also be able to search for manuscripts that have been owned by (say) the monastic society of the Lund cathedral. Or manuscripts that were illuminated in Italy.
  • Arkitektur http://sigge.lub.lu.se/2002/Laurentius/items.pdf
  • XML-baserat söksystem http://laurentius.lub.lu.se/search/presentation/laurentius.pdf

Urvalskriterier

  • Betalande "kunder": Digitalisering beställd av låntagare (externa såväl som interna). Frukterna inkorporeras i de digitala samlingarna
  • Material som är populärt: Digitalisering för att öka tillgänglighet och minska slitage
  • Material som ligger rätt i tiden (Laurentius kom till medan Masterprojektet var i gång och när Eva Nylander deltog i TEIs manuscript WG).
