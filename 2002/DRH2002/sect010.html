2. Typical functions in digital manuscript library

  • Medieval manuscripts are complicated
    • May contain well known content
    • Are unique entities. Pieces of art
  • Corresponding functional requirements for search engine: Ability to combine
    • Bibliographic search
    • Specific features of manuscripts
      • Scribe
      • Decoration
      • Former owners
      • ...
  • Capable cataloging scheme: Master XML DTD (http://www.tei-c.org/Master/Reference/)
  • Deliver full text. NB: Laurentius contains metadata and scanned images, no transcriptions
