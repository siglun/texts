A brief history.

The handling of theses at Lund university

1. Historical notes

  • Early focus (1996) on document data sheets
    • Automatic generation in postscript/pdf via TEX
    • Search engine for data sheets
  • Infrastructure for dissemination of dissertations, involving library staff and administrative staff at each faculty.
  • Legacy metadata attempting at both decent typography in HTML as well as in postscript and bibliographic description. Success as regards HTML, but only measured in 1995 design standards.
  • Status today: >2000 messy document data sheets, many of which are filled with exceptionally dirty HTML markup
  • Somehow, we made the right things, but in retrospect we realize that we did not always make them in the proper way.
