The handling of theses at Lund university

2. Archiving

  • Early initiative in archiving of dissertations through the Lund University Full Text (LUFT) project.
    • Currently > 100 objects in closed archive
    • Obligation to store at least 10 years.
    • Growing rapidly
    • Poor editorial infrastructure with far too much manual labour.
    • Current administrative procedures will not scale.
  • Archive uses PDF format.
  • About one third of all theses produced since 1998 has been archived in electronical form.
