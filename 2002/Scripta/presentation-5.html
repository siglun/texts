The handling of theses at Lund university

5. Future developments

  • To leave the terrible PDF format behind us. Search and navigation of documents in XML, as well as on demand generation of postscript and/or PDF from XML:
    • At the moment testing use of
      • XHTML (HTML as defined using XML rather than SGML)
      • TEI.
    • Will test DookBook DTD
  • We now own the university's printing workshop and can provide electronic as well as paper publication from the same XML source
  • Conserted actions.
    • Applying pressure upon publishers to permit full text publication
    • Establish common standards for metadata export and cross-searching
    • NDLTD -- so far we've been lazy, and just participated in some conferences
    • We are sub-contractors in FIGARO. EU project for Scholarly Communication and e-publishing. FIGARO implements peer review system.
    • OAI -- Studying implementations and methodologies. OAI harvesting is implemented in our article database ELIN, i.e., we can harvest OAI compliant databases.
