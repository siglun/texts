Wide area Harvesting

  • Typically a very large range of metadata elements. Raw data.
  • Large range of controlled vocabularies
  • The semantics used is fairly well defined within services and projects, but varies a lot between them
  • Typically very poor syntax, and unfortunately no generally accepted alternative
  • Fruits of syntactic ingenuity to remedy poor syntax is ofthen seen, but you cannot benefit from them.
