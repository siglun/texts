Aggravated autumn depression

Sigfrid Lundberg's Stuff 2009-10-15

The last few weeks I've not felt as good as I used to. As a matter of fact I've been really miserable.

It started in August. You see, when I am about to complete any substantial piece of work or project, I looked forward to the delivery with certain level of separation anxiety. And then, after all deliverables are completed and a new service is in place, I get a postpartum depression.

This autumn all this was aggravated by severe nicotine withdrawal symptoms. I'd say, don't even try giving up wet snuff, unless you've prepared yourself for a passage through hell.
