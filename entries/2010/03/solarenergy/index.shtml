Solar energy

Sigfrid Lundberg's Stuff 2010-03-08

White reflects radiation from the sun to a larger degree than do other colours. The small leaf, seems like oak, is absorbing enough energy to cause a local increase in temperature, which in turn has caused the melting of some snow.
