Recycled Votive Guitar on the Compost

Sigfrid Lundberg's Stuff 2010-04-06

I found this guitar at the cemetary where my father is buried. The instrument has been lying for a month or so on a grave together with reefs and other more traditional votives. Now it has been moved to the compost. For dust thou art, and unto dust shalt thou return.
