Ludvig Holberg

Ludvig Holberg on the main square in down-town Bergen, a wonderful city by a very Norwegian fjord beneath some Norwegian mountains. I took this snapshot when I visited Uni Digital

I wanted Ludvig, the square and the mountain on the same image. Ludvig isn't as sharp has he could have been. Sorry for that. The camera in my phone focused on the background.

Ludvig Holberg was born here, but his academic career is connected to the university of Copenhagen. The University of Bergen and Det Danske Sprog- og Litteraturselskab are now producing a new critical edition of Holbergs collected work. Uni Digital delivers the technology and then plan is that we, The Royal Library, Copenhagen, and the University Library, Bergen, share the burden of hosting the system.
